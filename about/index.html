Who We Are

ComplexCity lab was created in July 2013 between the 3 Universities of Technology in France (UTBM, UTC, UTT) and Shanghai University. The lab is lead by a binational steering committee and a scientific board.

What We Do

  • ComplexCity lab is an incubator for city related project using technology as a catalyst
  • Strategic Foresight for cities-related comprehensive research and international funding opportunities
  • Capture massive digital city data
  • Create & Design city-related visualizations and services
  • Think Tank about Smart Cities in France, Europe and China with interdisciplinary open events
  • Develop and Share methods and tools for Urban Complex-Systems Analysis

Our Process

  • 1We develop strategies and a platform for large scale dynamic heterogenous urban data capture (including traditional non digital data) and data high performance / high availability storage. We design simple end-point so that scientist from various disciplines can use them as simply as possible. This makes quantitative and qualitative data available to all our researchers and partners
  • 2We explore massive urban data using a wide range of data science techniques: machine learning (supervised and unsupervised classification), probabilist methods, design methodologies, 3D modeling, geographic and information visualization, language processing, serious gaming. We create methods and tools to be used by all lab partners.
  • 3We use the result of the two previous step to study complex interdisciplinary issues in the city: citizen health awareness, building of a smart green creative sustainable and lovable city, improving car traffic/walking conditions, modelization of places behaviors and characteristics, smart cctv use for security, livability and mobility.
  • 4We design online city services and serious games for citizens, companies and government based on our multi-layered data for them to explore, know better, and improve policies of their city. It also help in return crowd source urban data.

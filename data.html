Everything Geolocalized

We gather everything that mention a location, whether as a GPS coordinate couple, city name, address or textual location from any dataset, website, API that we found.

Everything is stored in many ways depending on the use of the data to maximise usability and efficiency at the same time.

Data usage

We do not share dataset online but we share how we got it. Check our repository on github to learn more about it.

If you wish to use some of our data or make a study with us, do not hesitate to contact us, we love hearing and developing new ideas.

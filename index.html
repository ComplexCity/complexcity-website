A COMPREHENSIVE SINO FRENCH LAB TO ADDRESS CRUCIAL CITY ISSUES

Understand the City

ComplexCity Lab is an interdisciplinary sino-french joint research laboratory. It's aims is to study the city, and to a greater extent, urban systems. We are taking an innovative approach which combines the analytical and computational capabilities of engineering sciences with the openness and creativeness of humanities and social sciences.

We consider the city as a complex system composed of a very large number of heterogeneous, dynamic, constantly mutative entities in non-linear interaction with each other at various scales. Exploring and analyzing this system leads us to technological, organizational and social innovations to enhance today's cities and help design the cities of tomorrow.

City Science

ComplexCity hosts 16 interdisciplinary research projects on cities which have at least one French and one Chinese researcher. In addition to these projects we are developing a conceptual framework based on data science aimed at creating a science of cities.

  1. We develop strategies and a platform for large scale dynamic heterogenous urban data capture (including traditional non digital data) and data high performance / high availability storage. We design simple end-point so that scientist from various disciplines can use them as simply as possible. This makes quantitative and qualitative data available to all our researchers and partners
  2. We explore massive urban data using a wide range of data science techniques: machine learning (supervised and unsupervised classification), probabilist methods, design methodologies, 3D modeling, geographic and information visualization, language processing, serious gaming. We create methods and tools to be used by all lab partners.
  3. We use the result of the two previous step to study complex interdisciplinary issues in the city: citizen health awareness, building of a smart green creative sustainable and lovable city, improving car traffic/walking conditions, modelization of places behaviors and characteristics, smart cctv use for security, livability and mobility.
  4. We design online city services and serious games for citizens, companies and government based on our multi-layered data for them to explore, know better, and improve policies of their city. It also help in return crowd source urban data.
